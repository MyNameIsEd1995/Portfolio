About Me

My academic path, technical evolution, and professional focus

Academic Background

As a Marine Data processor I have been deeply involved with geophysics and geohazard assessment for offshore operations, I help in processing several types of sonar data, extract insights from the results, and present them in a spatial view. Before initiating my career in the offshore and GIS industry I did an internship with SolidGround, part of Qeye, where I applied seimic inversion to extract relevant geotechnical properties from seismic signal. In 2019 and 2021 I graduated from my bachelors and masters degree in Geology, respectively, I took a minor in geotechnical engineering during my bachelors, but after moving to Denmark to pursue my masters degree, I did a couple of surveys and found myself drawn into the offshore world.

Interests

Geophysics | Climate Change | Terrestrial Environments | Geospatial Analysis | Conservation

This role pushed me to refine my technical toolkit:
